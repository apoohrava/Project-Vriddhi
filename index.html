About Us

About Us

Project Vriddhi is a social impact initiative started by a group of college students aiming at providing financial education to students, women and the marginalised communities. Our goal is to make people independent by spreading awareness, sharing knowledge, and making the financial literacy ecosystem more inclusive and relevant for all. The UNAI principle that we support is the commitment to educational opportunity for all regardless of gender, race, religion, or ethnicity as we encourage everyone to gain financial literacy and independence.
